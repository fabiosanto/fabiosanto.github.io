Quotemarks - read quote save

Quotemarks is every book reader companion app to save, keep track and share inspiring quotes from books you are reading to you friends on social networks.

- Scan you book isbn barcode to retrieve book's data

- Capture book's page and precisely select quote

- Choose theme and style of your quote card and share with your friends

Also, keep track of the book you are reading, relive inspiring quotes from books you have read in the past, explore suggested inspiring books shared by your friends.


Instructions

1. To scan you book press the + button on the bottom right of your screen

2. Scan your book's ISBN barcode to retrieve books cover, author and title

3. Capture your quote lines with the camera

4. Select precisely the lines you want to add

5. Review the result then press the done button
