Magicrop - No more one size fit them all!

No more one size fits all devices! With Magicrop you can easily crop your videos in the required frame format you need.

Play your video and move the frame across the area. Magicrop will crop exactly where the red cropping area was positioned while playing the video. When completed, export your video in all the formats you want. Quickly change the frame size to export in other formats.

Features:

  • Play to Crop - Magicrop records the cropping area while your video is playing. You can move the frame to crop different subjects and scenes as they move around the video.

  • Click to cut scene - Click on the video to quickly move the red frame to a different subject.

  • Drag to motion - Drag the red frame from start to end position to create motion cropping.

  • Size selector - Quickly change the format size to export in a different frame size. Magicrop will crop around the area previously selected.


Instructions

1. Select your video

2. Select the frame format you'd like to export to

3. Drag, Move, Click to move the red frame above the area you want to crop

4. Review if needed the result

5. Press on the Export button to export

6. Change format size to export in another format
